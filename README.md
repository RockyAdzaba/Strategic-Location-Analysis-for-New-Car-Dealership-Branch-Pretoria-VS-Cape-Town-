# Automotive Dealership Expansion Market Research  
**Cape Town vs Pretoria, South Africa | November 2025**

---

## About This Project

This repository contains a detailed market research study and presentation materials assessing the potential for opening a new car dealership branch in Cape Town or Pretoria. It covers market size, economic and demographic analysis, competitive landscape, regulatory environment, financial projections, SWOT and PESTL analyses.

---

## Research Objectives

- Compare market potential of Cape Town and Pretoria  
- Analyze 2024-2025 automotive industry trends  
- Evaluate demographics, income, vehicle ownership, infrastructure  
- Map competitive landscape and customer behavior  
- Assess regulations, licensing, and business environment  
- Conduct SWOT and risk analyses for each city  
- Deliver data-driven location recommendation with ROI forecasts  
- Provide investor-ready presentation and implementation guide

## Key Findings

- **Pretoria** recommended as optimal location (Weighted Score: 76.1/100 vs Cape Town 67.5/100)  
- Pretoria advantages: larger market (3-4.1M population), manufacturing hub (40% vehicle production), 35-40% lower operating costs, government R60B+ investment, favorable BEE incentives  
- Cape Town advantages: business-friendly regulatory environment, higher consumer income (~R47K+), superior digital infrastructure, strategic port logistics, strong premium brand market  
- Financial outlook: 5-year ROI of 18-42% (Pretoria) vs 12-30% (Cape Town)  
- Break-even expected within 24-30 months (Pretoria)

## PESTL Analysis Highlights

- Political: Pretoria has higher government manufacturing support but faces administrative delays, Cape Town offers stable governance and regulatory predictability.  
- Economic: Pretoria benefits from manufacturing incentives and cost advantages, Cape Town benefits from a higher income consumer base and diversified economy.  
- Social: Pretoria offers strong government employment and fleet sales opportunities; Cape Town presents a more affluent, lifestyle-driven consumer market.  
- Technological: Cape Town leads in digital infrastructure and e-commerce adoption, Pretoria offers manufacturing IT system integration possibilities.  
- Legal: Cape Town features streamlined licensing (20-45 days); Pretoria has longer licensing timelines (60-90 days) but offers tax incentives and BEE preferences.
